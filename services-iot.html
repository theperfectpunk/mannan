IoT/Devices Platform

From its inception, was founded for one sole purpose, to enable the Internet of things. Dedicated to solving the idiosyncrasies of developing and managing connected devices, Mannan provides a number of products that enable customers to quickly connect any device, to the cloud and applications, and make meaningful decisions from their data. Mannan's Agile IoT Platform, Agile Mobile Application Platform, and Mannan Insights, provides device manufacturers with the connectivity, security, and feedback loop needed to create better products and more engaging user experiences
